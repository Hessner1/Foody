## 🤔 What is Foody?

Point your camera at a barcode and Foody instantly tells you the things a label
makes hard to see:

- **Is it safe for *me*?** — a traffic‑light verdict against *your* allergen profile.
- **What are these E‑numbers?** — every additive named, classified and explained.
- **Does it fit my diet?** — checked against the diets you follow.
- **What's the nutrition?** — calories & macros, per 100 g and per package.

No account needed. No ads. Your data stays on your device unless *you* decide to back it up.

## ✨ Features

| | |
|---|---|
| 📷 **Instant barcode scan** | Real‑time, on‑device camera scanning — frames never leave your phone. |
| 🚨 **Allergen safety gate** | Matches EU‑14 allergens **and** your own free‑text ones, with a recall‑first "when unsure, warn" design. |
| 🧪 **Additives, decoded** | E‑codes pulled straight from the ingredient list, each with its name, function, class and plain‑language effect. |
| 🥬 **Diet checks** | Vegan, vegetarian, halal, kosher and more — flagged right on the result screen. |
| 🎯 **"Fits You" panel** | A personal read on whether a product matches your body goals & preferences. |
| 🔢 **Nutrition & scores** | Calories, proteins, fats, carbs, Nutri‑Score and NOVA group. |
| 📔 **Food diary & calorie goals** | Log portions and track your day. |
| 🍳 **Dish / recipe builder** | Combine scanned ingredients into a dish with exact per‑portion nutrition. |
| 📱 **Offline‑first** | Everything works on‑device; the cloud is optional. |
| ☁️ **Optional sync** | Sign in to back up and sync your profile, diary and dishes — and delete it all anytime. |
| 🌍 **Multilingual** | Ukrainian, English and Russian. |

## 🔐 Privacy in one line

> **No ads. No trackers. No advertising ID. No third‑party analytics.**
> Your diary, dishes, allergens and history live on your device by default. Sign‑in
> is optional and only backs up *your* data to *your* private space — deletable at
> any time, in the app or [on the web](https://foody-alpha-seven.vercel.app/delete-account.html).

## 📊 Data sources

Product information (names, ingredients, nutrition, images) comes primarily from
**Foody's own product database**, which we build and maintain, and is
**supplemented by [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org)** — an open,
crowdsourced food database — to fill in products we don't yet cover.

> Where Open Food Facts data is used: product data © Open Food Facts contributors,
> licensed under the **Open Database License (ODbL) v1.0**. Individual contents
> (e.g. photos) may be under CC‑BY‑SA.

Optional account backup & sync are powered by **Google Firebase** (Authentication +
Cloud Firestore), used purely for *your* data — never for ads, profiling or analytics.
